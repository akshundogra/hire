ABM job search automation

The full system,
end to end.

Instead of sending a CV, send a personalised landing page built specifically for each company you're applying to — generated automatically with real SEO data, funding research, and AI‑written content.

What This Does

  1. Crawls their website with Screaming Frog — real SEO data (missing alt text, broken links, title lengths).
  2. Looks up their funding stage via DuckDuckGo — adjusts tone (Seed vs Series A vs Series B+).
  3. Scans their privacy policy — detects their marketing stack (HubSpot, Salesforce, LinkedIn Ads etc.).
  4. Generates a personalised page with Claude — hero headline, 90‑day plan, 3‑angle audit, why them.
  5. Publishes to GitHub Pages — live at hire.YOURDOMAIN.com/[company-slug].
  6. Generates a custom OG image — “HI [COMPANY], I HAVE A PROPOSITION” burned into your GIF.
  7. Writes outreach copy to a CSV — email subject, full email body, LinkedIn note, LinkedIn message.

The result: a link you send instead of a CV that shows the company you actually did your homework.

  ├─► Prepare Prompt (Code)     Assembles Claude prompt with all research data

  └─► Flask /save-outreach      Appends row to ~/Desktop/ABM-Outreach-Tracker.csv
